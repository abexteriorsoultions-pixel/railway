Questions Customers Ask

Fast Answers Before You Book

Clear expectations make the whole job smoother, from the first click to the final rinse.

How does booking work?

Choose your service, date, and 2-hour slot. The appointment is saved as pending, added to our calendar, and confirmed by the team.

Are Sundays available?

No. Sundays are kept off the online schedule. Saturday appointments are available from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Can property managers book?

Yes. We work with homeowners, renters, landlords, and property managers who need dependable exterior cleaning or waste removal.

What if my job needs a custom quote?

Use the booking form and choose Waste Removal or Exterior Refresh. Add details and photos later if needed, and we will confirm pricing before work starts.
